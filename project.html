The Kawasaki Z900 is a standard motorcycle of the Kawasaki Z series made by Kawasaki since 2017. It replaced the Z800.
It is the flagship Z series motorcycle besides the ZH2 from Kawasaki.The Kawasaki Z900 is priced at Rs 8,93,000 (ex-showroom India), and is available
in Metallic Phantom Black / Metallic Carbon Grey and Ebony / Metallic Matte Graphene Steel Grey colours. The Kawasaki Z900 is powered by a 948cc,
inline-four cylinder engine that produces 125PS and 98.6Nm of peak torque.

The Z900 epitomizes Kawasaki's belief of what the ideal supernaked should be. With a 948cc engine and ultra-lightweight chassis, every ride is met
with exceptional power, responsiveness and excitement. Make your presence known on the streets with signature Sugomi™ inspired styling and a commanding
ride position.

DESIGN

The crouching stance, low-positioned head and upswept tail design cues of the bike follow the Japanese ‘Sugomi’ design ideaology.
Sugomi translates to a person, animal or thing that has a superior aura. The aggressive and brute stance of this bike looks as intimidating as it performs.
This streetfighter is available in two colour schemes, Metallic Spark Black and a more vibrant Candy Lime Green Type 3.
